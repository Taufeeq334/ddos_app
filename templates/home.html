About the Project

A Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attack is a malicious attempt to disrupt the normal traffic of a network by overwhelming the target with a flood of Internet traffic. These attacks can cause severe service disruptions and financial losses.

Our deep learning-based DDoS Detection System leverages artificial intelligence to identify patterns in network traffic and detect anomalies that indicate an ongoing attack. By using a trained neural network model, our system can classify incoming traffic as normal or malicious in real-time, helping organizations stay protected.

Key Features:

  • Real-time traffic monitoring and anomaly detection
  • Deep learning model trained on large-scale datasets
  • Automated mitigation strategies to prevent service disruption
  • Scalable and adaptable to different network environments

With our system, organizations can take a proactive approach to network security, reducing the risks associated with DDoS attacks.
